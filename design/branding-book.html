03 — Logo

The louvered mark

Stacked blades echo the product's adjustable roof. Pair the mark with the uppercase wordmark, and give it room to breathe.

PERGOLACAVE
PERGOLACAVE
PERGOLACAVE

Do

  • Keep clear space equal to the mark's height on all sides.
  • Use the espresso mark on light surfaces, orange or white on dark.
  • Maintain the uppercase, tightly-tracked wordmark.

Don't

  • Stretch, rotate, or recolor the blades arbitrarily.
  • Place the mark on a busy photo without a scrim.
  • Mix the wordmark with any other typeface.

05 — Voice & Tone

How we sound

Direct, warm, and proof-driven. We talk like a knowledgeable installer — confident about the product, never hyped or salesy.

Direct.

Say it plainly. Lead with the benefit, skip the fluff.

Warm.

Friendly and human. We're helping neighbors upgrade their backyard.

Proof-driven.

Back claims with specs, materials, and real numbers.

Do write

  • "Built from powder-coated aluminum. Rated for LA sun and rain."
  • "In stock and ready to ship — free delivery."

Don't write

  • "The most AMAZING pergola you'll EVER own!!!"
  • "Limited time mega-blowout — act now or miss out forever."
