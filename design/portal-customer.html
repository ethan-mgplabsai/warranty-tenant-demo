Trail Supply Co. Warranty Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Trail Supply Co. stands behind the quality of every product we sell. Our warranty covers manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship under normal use for the duration specified for each product category.

Coverage by Product Category

Category Duration Resolution Return Required
Backpacks (Osprey) 36 months or 5,000 miles Replace Part Yes
Grills 48 months Repair Yes
Cookware 24 months Replace Full No
All other products 12 months Refund No

What's Covered

  • Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
  • Mechanical and electrical component failures under normal use
  • Structural integrity issues (seams, zippers, buckles, welds)

What's Not Covered

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage caused by misuse, abuse, or negligence
  • Unauthorized modifications or repairs
  • Cosmetic damage that does not affect functionality
  • Products purchased from unauthorized retailers

How to File a Claim

  1. Navigate to My Claims and select "File a Claim"
  2. Select the registered product you are filing a claim for
  3. Describe the issue and upload photos of the defect
  4. Our team will review your claim within 2 business days
  5. If approved, we will process the resolution (repair, replacement, or refund) and provide return instructions if applicable

Conditions

  • Products must be registered within 30 days of purchase to be eligible for warranty coverage
  • Proof of purchase is required (your order is automatically linked during registration)
  • When a return is required, the item must be shipped within 14 days of claim approval
  • Warranty coverage begins on the date of purchase and is non-transferable
